Electricity Consumption Analysis

Our project focuses on the detailed analysis of electricity consumption in 2019 and 2020 using Tableau, taking into account various states across India.

By examining the provided dataset, which includes information on states, regions, latitude, longitude, dates, and usage, we gained valuable insights into energy consumption patterns and trends.
